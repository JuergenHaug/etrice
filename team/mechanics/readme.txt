Workspace Mechanic
==================

This plug-in helps to manage preferences and keep them in sync over several work spaces and users.

https://code.google.com/a/eclipselabs.org/p/workspacemechanic/

The files in this directory are a selection of preference takss for the eTrice project.

Note:
For the comment templates to work nicely we recommend to add your full user name at the end of your eclipse.ini

[...]
-vmargs
-Dosgi.requiredJavaVersion=1.6
-Xms40m
-Xmx512m
-Duser.name=Your full name
